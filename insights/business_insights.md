# Business Insights – Coffee Shop Sales (Excel Dashboard)

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: $698,812
- Total Footfall: 149,116
- Average Bill per Person: 4.69
- Average Orders per Person: 1.44

## Insights
- Peak sales occur between **8 AM – 10 AM**
- Coffee contributes the largest share of revenue
- Large and Regular sizes dominate customer preference
- Hell's Kitchen store generates highest sales
- Weekdays outperform weekends in total orders

## Business Recommendations
- Increase staffing during morning hours
- Promote coffee combos and upsell larger sizes
- Focus marketing on weekdays
- Optimize inventory based on top-selling products
